Summer Plans Decision Analysis

Timeframe: February 2023 - April 2023


Technology Used: Microsoft Excel


Link:

Google Slides

This was a group term project I worked on for the course 88-223 - Decision Analysis at Carnegie Mellon University in the Spring 2023 Semester. In this project, we identified some choices for how Brady - our classmate and group member - should spend his summer break, and determined the best ones using adjusted cost-benefit and sensitivity analyses based on factors such as finances, enjoyment, and usefulness of the choice to his future. We determined that the best way for Brady to spend his summer break would be to work at his internship at Payliance. If that and all of his other internship offers fall through, then his next best option would be to work at the golf course in his hometown.
